Global Insurance

Global Insurance started as a simple online health and life insurance web project at K L University. At Global Insurance, we strive to provide the best health and life Insurance plans for you, from the convenience of your home without the need to contact anyone. With the events of the recent pandemic, our mission shined more relevant and necessary.

  • Through us you you need not wait to apply or for the application to be processed or to pay your dues or to receive your amount. All this seamlessly happens through our user friendly web application where you don't have to rely on anyone for either of the steps.
  • Most of the frauds happening in the insurance industry are due to the agents. Here you need not worry about such scams as here you do not rely on other people for either of the steps, keeping you on top of every step of the process.
  • Who said that insuring yourself or your loved ones has to be a time consuming process, with our web application your insurance is one less thing for you to worry about.
  • With Global Insurance being a web-application you can access your portfolio, pay your dues, get new insurance around the world, around the clock.
